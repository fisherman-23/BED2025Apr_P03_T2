Welcome to Chair yoga

Chair yoga adapts yoga for seated practice, enhancing flexibility, strength, and mindfulness.

At the end of this exercise, you should feel a sense of increased calm and reduced stress. Experience improved flexibility and range of motion in your joints.Notice enhanced circulation throughout your body.
